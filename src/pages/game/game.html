About the Game

Smash your way to victory in Breakout! Bounce the ball, clear the bricks, and power up for an epic arcade experience!

How to Play

Control the Paddle:
  • Use your fingers to move the paddle on touch devices.
  • Control the paddle by dragging with your mouse pointer on desktop.
  • Alternatively, navigate the paddle using the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard.

To win you need to destroy all the bricks.

You lose upon the ball pass under the paddle.

You can play the game as many as you want!
